
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
% USE vshadow TO CREATE SHADOW COPY
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

Executable: C:\Program Files\Microsoft SDKs\Windows\v7.0\Bin\x64\vsstools\vshadow.exe
(Run as administrator)

Commands:
vshadow -p -nw -script=SETVAR_tmp.cmd
call SETVAR_tmp.cmd
vshadow -el=%SHADOW_ID_1%,D:\temp\shadow

Do backup magic reading from shadow copy!

vshadow -ds=%SHADOW_ID_1%
